Cricket Atlas — Privacy Policy

Cricket Atlas ("the app", "we", "us") is a cricketer name-chain game. This policy explains what information the app handles, why, and the choices you have. By using Cricket Atlas — on the web at arkapyd.github.io/cricatlas or as an installed app — you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Google account details. When you sign in with Google, we receive your Google display name and a unique account identifier (UID). We do not receive your Google password.
  • Gameplay data. We store your Career Points (CP), rank, and related game progress, associated with your account UID.
  • Local device storage. The app stores small amounts of data on your device (for example, settings and offline caching) so it works offline and remembers your preferences.
  • Automatically collected data. Standard technical data such as device type, app version, and basic usage/diagnostic information may be collected by the services listed below.

How we use it

  • To sign you in and show your name.
  • To track and display your career progression and unlock game modes.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the game.
  • To show advertising that helps keep the game free.

We do not sell your personal information.

Third-party services

Cricket Atlas relies on the following third parties, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication and Realtime Database) — sign-in and storage of your game data. See the Firebase privacy documentation and Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense / Google AdMob — advertising. These services may use cookies and device identifiers to serve and personalize ads and measure their performance. See How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.
  • Wikipedia and ESPNcricinfo — the app looks up cricketer names to verify moves. Only the cricketer name being checked is sent in these lookups; no personal information about you is included.

Advertising choices

Ads may be personalized based on cookies or your device's advertising identifier. You can control or reset this: on the web via Google My Ad Center, and on Android via Settings → Google → Ads (to reset or opt out of your advertising ID). Where required by law, you will be asked for consent before personalized ads are shown.

Data retention and deletion

  • You can reset your career progress at any time from the app's Settings, which sets your CP back to zero.
  • To request deletion of your account data, email [YOUR-EMAIL] from the address linked to your Google account, and we will remove your stored game data.

Children

Cricket Atlas is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us and we will delete it.

Security

We use Google Firebase's infrastructure and access rules to protect your data. No method of storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to safeguard your information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above, and continued use of the app after changes means you accept the revised policy.
